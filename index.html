Visit Setúbal, Portugal

Enjoy this charming city surrounded by mountains, beaches, and ancient history.

Top three activities to do in Setúbal

Spend a day at the beach

Where Parque Natural da Arrábida meets the coastline, are some of the most beautiful beaches in the world.

Mercado do Livramento

Buy some fish that was caught just a few hours ago, local produce and cheeses, and delicious baked goods in this bustling pulic market.

Castelo de Palmela

Just a few km north of Setúbal lies this 12th century fort that offers panoramic views of the region.
